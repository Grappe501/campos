# Volunteer Command — accountability engine

**Accountability** here means **clarity + support**, not punishment. The accountability engine connects **signals**, **nudges**, **human follow-up**, and **reassignment** so volunteers stay **in motion** or **dignifiedly pause**—without public shame or automated guilt.

Aligned with `behavior-model.md`, `state-machine.md`, and `culture-and-moderation.md`. No code or SQL.

---

## Guided accountability

**Definition:** Expectations are **visible** (tasks, cadence, group norms); **owners** are known (self, captain, staff); **escalation** is **scripted**—Steve and product **guide**, humans **decide** on sensitive outcomes.

**Components**

- **Clear commitments** — tasks with realistic time cost; lanes with honest descriptions.  
- **Private first** — missed commitments addressed in **DM / private notes** before any broader visibility.  
- **Documented paths** — pause, reassign, needs follow-up—never dead ends.

---

## Gentle nudges

**Purpose:** Reduce silent drop-off; **never** replace human judgment on conflict or safety.

**Characteristics**

- **Low frequency** — pacing respects `pacing-engine.md`; no notification storms.  
- **Contextual** — different copy for **first miss** vs **pattern** vs **post-pause return**.  
- **Steve-assisted** — suggests one next step; offers **smaller** task alternatives.

**Forbidden**

- Public “you failed” messages.  
- Escalating shame language.  
- Infinite automated loops without human queue when **needs follow-up** triggers.

---

## Adaptive reassignment

**When:** Misfit, life change, burnout risk, or **missed tasks** after private check-in.

**Sequence (default)**

1. **Private check-in** — human or approved Steve template.  
2. **Resize** — smaller or later task; digital swap if in-person failed.  
3. **Redirect lane** — new team or geography with **volunteer consent**.  
4. **Pause** — explicit reduced asks (`paused` state).  
5. **Inactive / alumni** — only after policy window or explicit choice—**redirect, not discard** of human worth; inactive is **state**, not insult.

**Product**

- **Reassignment** creates a **clear record** of who owns the new scope (for dashboards)—without exposing private reasons in feeds.

---

## Reliability tracking

**Purpose:** Give **captains and staff** an honest view of **follow-through** for planning—without turning volunteers into scores worn in public.

**Conceptual metrics (internal)**

- Task completion rate within lane.  
- **Streaks** of participation (optional, private-first).  
- **No-show** patterns for shifts (with excused vs unexcused where captured).  
- **Responsiveness** to captain messages (bounded—avoid creepy surveillance).

**Use**

- **Staff/captain dashboards** — staffing decisions, who needs coaching.  
- **Not** volunteer-facing leaderboards of “reliability rank” by default.

---

## Dashboard surfacing for human follow-up

**Queues the product should support conceptually**

| Queue | Meaning | Owner |
|-------|---------|--------|
| **Needs follow-up** | Confusion, conflict, accessibility, failed onboarding | Staff / designated captain |
| **At-risk** | Engagement drop early warning | Captain → staff |
| **Pattern misses** | Repeated missed tasks after private nudge | Captain |
| **Permission mismatch** | Role says captain but training incomplete | Staff |
| **Pause return** | Ready to resume, needs re-placement | Captain / staff |

**Principle:** If a machine can detect **at-risk**, a **human** should see it on a **dashboard** before automation escalates tone.

---

## No punitive framing

**Copy and UX**

- **“We missed you”** not **“You’re behind.”**  
- **“Let’s find a fit”** not **“You’re underperforming.”**  
- **States** are descriptive (`at-risk`, `paused`)—never moral labels in user-visible strings.

**Product**

- **No** fines, demerits, or public demotion rituals.  
- **Step-down** from leadership is **private** and **dignified** with clear permission rollback.

---

## Redirect rather than discard

Every exit path from a misfit should offer:

- **Another lane** — digital, relational, events, behind-the-scenes.  
- **Pause** — no guilt.  
- **Alumni / supporter-only** — stay in relationship without organizing load.

**Steve** explicitly frames **redirect** as success when someone finds a sustainable role.
