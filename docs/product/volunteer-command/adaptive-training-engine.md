# Volunteer Command — adaptive training engine

Training is **not** a content dump—it is **unlocking competence at the moment of need** and **preparing the next lane** without forcing everyone through the same curriculum. This document defines **task-based** and **pathway-based** training, **just-in-time** delivery, **unlocks**, and **anticipation** of upcoming skills.

Structured for future mapping to **modules**, **gates**, **assignments**, and **Steve prompts**—no code or SQL.

---

## Principles

- **Just-in-time** beats “complete 40 hours before you do anything.”  
- **Unlocks** tie training to **risk**—voter contact, data access, leadership scope—not to bureaucracy.  
- **Pathways** show **where someone might go**, not mandatory promotion.  
- **Refreshers** appear when **rules change** or **high-risk windows** approach (e.g., Election Week).

---

## Task-based training

**Definition:** Short learning tied to **a concrete action** the volunteer is about to take or just failed at safely.

**Examples**

- Before first canvass: **safety + legal basics** micro-module.  
- Before sending peer messages at scale: **consent + script** acknowledgment.  
- Before handling sensitive lists: **data handling** module.

**Behavior**

- **Gate:** task remains **locked** or **escorted** until completion—or **human waiver** with audit where policy allows.  
- **Steve** can surface **one** module at a time next to the task—no wall of tabs.

---

## Pathway-based training

**Definition:** Sequences for **lanes** or **leadership tiers**—captain path, relational lead, digital comms helper—with **ordered** competencies.

**Structure (conceptual)**

- **Path** — e.g., “Captain readiness.”  
- **Milestone** — cluster of modules + **practice** (e.g., co-lead a shift with supervision).  
- **Completion** — unlocks **declared leadership** promotion workflow (`leadership-system.md`).

**Behavior**

- **Visible** to volunteers who opt into the path—**not** forced on steady-lane contributors.  
- **Progress** private by default; **badges** opt-in for public feeds.

---

## Just-in-time delivery

**Triggers**

- **Upcoming task** with risk class.  
- **First time** in a new geography or program.  
- **Compliance window** opens (disclosure rules, training mandated by partner).  
- **Failure signal** — e.g., QC flags from field (human-triggered follow-up training, not auto-blame).

**Anti-patterns**

- Assigning long irrelevant courses because “everyone gets the same folder.”  
- Training **after** harm—use sparingly; **prevention** is default.

---

## Training unlocks

**Unlock types (conceptual)**

| Unlock | Unlocks |
|--------|---------|
| **Task class** | Specific task types (canvass, peer SMS batch, data export). |
| **Role** | Captain tools, moderation, subtree visibility. |
| **Phase** | High-intensity window behaviors (election protection basics). |
| **Compliance** | Partner or jurisdictional requirements. |

**Governance**

- **Staff** defines which unlocks attach to which risk classes (`control-layer.md`).  
- **Audit** when unlocks are granted by **exception**.

---

## Anticipation of next-needed skills

**Purpose:** Reduce surprise and anxiety—**Steve** and pathways surface **what’s coming** if the volunteer moves toward a new lane or leadership step.

**Signals**

- Volunteer **expressed interest** in captaincy or new lane.  
- **Captain** flagged readiness.  
- **Campaign phase** shifts (`pacing-engine.md`)—e.g., GOTV intensity.

**Behaviors**

- **Preview modules** — optional, short.  
- **Suggested order** — “when you’re ready, next is…”  
- **No** automatic assignment of heavy training without **consent** or **role need**.

---

## Linking training to current task and future lane

**Dual link**

- **Current task:** training item embedded in **task detail** (why this matters, 3-minute video, acknowledge).  
- **Future lane:** pathway shows **dependencies**—e.g., “To lead events, complete A + B + observed shift.”

**Handoff**

- **Training completion** events feed **leadership pipeline** dashboards (`leadership-system.md`).  
- **Incomplete training** blocks **permission elevation**—never silently.

---

## Steve’s role

- **Surfaces** the next module at the right moment; **does not** pass compliance tests on behalf of users.  
- **Routes** to humans when training touches **legal uncertainty** or **accommodations**.

---

## Dashboard hooks

- **Staff:** completion rates by module, **bottlenecks**, **expiring** certifications if used.  
- **Captains:** who on their team is **cleared** for which actions—**before** assigning work.  
- **Volunteers:** personal **training log** and **what’s unlocked**.
